					[Sending messages](/docs/send-single-facebook-messenger-message) to your users over API is quite simple. When it comes to [receiving incoming messages](/docs/receive-facebook-message) from your users, you can choose between two methods: * **Http forward method** - allows you to receive messages from your subscribers in real-time * **Pull method** - allows you to fetch new messages from Infobip server over API at your convenience You can choose your preferable method by configuring Action.
